Welcome to Book eXchange

A peer to peer book transfer ecosystem

As a group of students it is a struggle in the beginning of the semester to know the books required and find it at a decent price. So we want to make an easy to use portal for students to use to get the books they need for a semester.

  • Learn More

How do we work

Search for Books

Search for Books required for different classes in your university and find the information for the books required for a particular class/professor.

Buy Books

Go ahead and buy the text books from the market place offered by us. Our market connects students who are selling the books and if they are not available, you can always be transferred to Amazon

Sell your Book

Selling your text books is easier than ever. Post your information of the book and we will do our best to get the book connected to the corresponding class and allow users to buy it.

Rent Books

If you love your text book but do not want to part away with it, we allow you to rent your text book to our other users for a given price.

Supported Universities

Georgia State University

We are currently supporting Georgia State University with it's classes and the list of books required. So students who are intersted are welcome to join us.
